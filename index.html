R&D Software Engineer

Mohammad Samadi is a researcher at Polytechnic Institute of Porto, an external collaborator at INESC TEC, and a PhD candidate at UPC-BarcelonaTech. He has worked in industry as a software engineer to mainly develop information systems on different platforms. His research interests include software architecture, parallel programming, distributed computing, and artificial intelligence. He has served as a referee and reviewer in scientific journals and conferences, in addition to being a lecturer of university and an advisor to graduate students.
