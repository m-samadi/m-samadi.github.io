Bio

Mohammad Samadi is a development researcher at Polytechnic Institute of Porto and a PhD candidate at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya – BarcelonaTech. He has worked in companies as a software engineer to develop various information systems. Mohammad has worked in research groups as a development researcher to improve software architecture and intelligent computing of cyber-physical systems. He was also a lecturer of university and an advisor to graduate students.
